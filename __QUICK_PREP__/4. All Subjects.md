## SDLC Lifecycle
### 🧠 REQUIREMENT & PLANNING
-   ** How do you handle ambiguous requirements? ? **
-   ** How do you convert business requirements → technical design? ? **
-   ** How do you estimate tasks accurately? ? **
-   ** Ever challenged product requirements? Example? ? **
-   ** How do you prioritize when everything is “urgent”? ? **
-   👉 Trap: Saying “product decides” = weak. You must show pushback + influence

### 🏗️ DESIGN & ARCHITECTURE
-   ** Walk me through your system design approach ? **
-   ** How do you ensure scalability & reliability? ? **
-   ** Trade-offs you made in a design decision ? **
-   ** How do you review design of others? ? **
-   ** Example of bad design you fixed ? **
-   👉 Expect deep probing: caching, DB, concurrency, failure handling

### 💻 DEVELOPMENT
-   ** How do you ensure code quality? ? **
-   ** Your code review philosophy ? **
-   ** Handling legacy code / refactoring strategy ? **
-   ** How do you enforce coding standards? ? **
-   ** Example where you improved dev productivity ? **
-   👉 Trap: Saying “we use Sonar” = surface-level answer

### 🧪 TESTING STRATEGY
-   ** How do you define a testing strategy? ? **
-   ** Unit vs integration vs e2e — what do YOU enforce? ? **
-   ** How do you handle flaky tests? ? **
-   ** How do you ensure coverage actually matters? ? **
-   👉 They want ownership, not “QA team handles it”

### 🚀 RELEASE / DEPLOYMENT
-   ** Describe your release process ? **
-   ** How do you handle zero-downtime deployments? ? **
-   ** Rollback strategy? ? **
-   ** Blue-Green vs Canary — when to use what? ? **
-   ** Handling failed deployments ? **
-   👉 If you haven’t owned releases → problem

### 📊 PRODUCTION & MONITORING
-   ** How do you monitor systems in production? ? **
-   ** What metrics do you track? (p95 latency, error rate, etc.) ? **
-   ** Alerting strategy ? **
-   ** Handling production incidents (step-by-step) ? **
-   👉 Must show calm + structured response

### 🔥 INCIDENT MANAGEMENT (CRITICAL)
-   ** Walk me through a SEV1 incident you handled ? **
-   ** Root cause analysis — how do you do it? ? **
-   ** Preventing recurrence ? **
-   ** Postmortem process ? **
-   👉 Trap: No clear structure = reject

### 🔄 MAINTENANCE & IMPROVEMENT
-   ** How do you handle tech debt? ? **
-   ** Balancing feature vs stability ? **
-   ** Continuous improvement examples ? **
-   ** Sunset/decommission strategy ? **

### 👥 PROCESS & TEAM
-   ** Agile vs Scrum vs Kanban — what actually works? ? **
-   ** How do you run sprint planning? ? **
-   ** Handling missed commitments ? **
-   ** Cross-team dependencies ? **
-   👉 Don’t give textbook answers — give what you actually enforce

### 📈 METRICS & DELIVERY
-   ** How do you measure team success? ? **
-   ** DORA metrics (VERY important) ? **
-   ** Deployment frequency ? **
-   ** Lead time ? **
-   ** MTTR ? **
-   ** Change failure rate ? **
-   ** How did you improve these? ? **
-   👉 If you don’t know DORA → you’re behind

## Behavioral
### 🔥 OWNERSHIP / LEADERSHIP
-   ** Tell me about a system you owned end-to-end ? **
-   ** Describe a time you took responsibility beyond your role ? **
-   ** Biggest failure in production — what did YOU do ? **
-   ** When did you make a decision without enough data ? **
-   ** A time you disagreed with leadership and what happened ? **
-   👉 Trap: If you say “team did…”, you’re done.
### ⚔️ CONFLICT / STAKEHOLDER MANAGEMENT
-   ** Tell me about a conflict with a teammate ? **
-   ** Conflict with product/business — how did you handle it ? **
-   ** When someone underperformed — what did you do ? **
-   ** Handling toxic team member / strong ego engineer ? **
-   ** Ever escalated an issue? Why ? **
-   👉 Trap: Saying “we resolved it amicably” without specifics = weak.
### 🚨 FAILURE / INCIDENT HANDLING
-   ** Describe a production outage you handled ? **
-   ** Biggest mistake in your career ? **
-   ** A decision that backfired ? **
-   ** Missed deadline — what went wrong ? **
-   👉 Trap: Blaming others = instant rejection.
### 🧠 DECISION MAKING
-   ** Toughest technical decision you made ? **
-   ** Trade-offs: performance vs scalability vs cost ? **
-   ** When did you say NO to a feature ? **
-   ** How do you prioritize under pressure ? **
-   👉 They want structured thinking, not gut feeling.

### 👥 MENTORSHIP / TEAM BUILDING
-   ** How do you mentor junior engineers ? **
-   ** Example where you improved team productivity ? **
-   ** Handling low performer ? **
-   ** Hiring decision you made ? **
-   👉 Trap: If you haven’t mentored, you’re not senior. ? **

### 🚀 IMPACT / EXECUTION
-   ** Biggest impact you created in last 1–2 years ? **
-   ** How did you improve a system/process significantly ? **
-   ** Delivered something under tight deadline ? **
-   ** Reduced cost / improved performance — HOW exactly ? **
-   👉 Numbers matter. No metrics = weak answer. ? **

### 🧩 AMBIGUITY / OWNERSHIP
-   ** Worked on unclear requirements — what did you do ? **
-   ** Built something from scratch ? **
-   ** Dealt with changing priorities ? **

### 🏢 CULTURE / VALUES (company-specific twist)
-   ** Why this company ? **
-   ** Why leaving current role ? **
-   ** What motivates you now ? **
-   ** What kind of team do you want ? **
-   👉 Trap: Generic answers = rejection.
